Photosynthetic Arts is a collective of artists based on photography, videography and webdesign. Thereby is the focus on landscaping-, nature-, lifestyle- and eventphotography.

We have discovered creative spirits in our beloved sphere and their productions were a key to create our first collective projects. Stay tuned!

We are very pleased for a common collaboration with you, just get in touch with us.
